I wandered Lonly as a Cloud

William Wordsworth
          For oft,
        on my           couch I lie In                            vacant       or
     pensive      mood,               They       flash
     upon that        inward eye hich is      the bliss of
                      solitude; And then               my heart
    with pleasure fills,                               And          dances with
     the          daffodils.
